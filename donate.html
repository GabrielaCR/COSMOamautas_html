Bring the universe to a rural classroom in Peru

Your gift helps a rural school teacher open an astronomy club — sparking a love of science and the stars in more than 50 children.

In the Andes, watching the sky is a heritage centuries old. At CosmoAmautas, we keep that heritage alive: we train and empower teachers in rural schools across Peru to open astroclubs — spaces where astronomy becomes a doorway to science, curiosity, and new STEM opportunities for their students. We believe that exploring and understanding the universe should never be a privilege for just a few.

Contribute what you can Every gift counts.

What does your donation achieve?

Your donation adds to a shared goal. For every US$100 we raise together, we make a complete astroclub possible. With that amount, a rural school teacher receives:

Training to teach astronomy and lead their astroclub.

A full set of materials, shipped directly to their school — no matter how remote.

Three months of close mentoring from our team.

The result: a brand-new astroclub reaching at least 50 students in rural areas.
